History

TechCon was founded in 2001 in response to the rapid advancements in technology and the burgeoning interest in the tech sector following the dot-com boom. The first event was organized by a small group of tech entrepreneurs and industry professionals who aimed to create a platform for sharing ideas, showcasing new technologies, and fostering networking opportunities. The inaugural conference featured a modest lineup of speakers, primarily local experts, and a few keynote presentations from industry leaders. The primary focus was on software development, internet innovations, and the impact of emerging technologies on businesses. Despite its humble beginnings, the conference quickly gained traction, attracting attention from media and industry stakeholders.

Misssion

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to advance the technology sector by empowering innovators, facilitating knowledge exchange, and building a diverse and inclusive community. The conference aims to foster collaboration among industry professionals, promote responsible technology practices, and address sustainability challenges. Through engaging sessions and networking opportunities, TechCon 2024 seeks to create a transformative experience that drives innovation and positive impact in the tech industry.
